Aspects of Gene Therapy

Introduction

Gene therapy modifies genes inside the body to treat or cure diseases. It aims to fix genetic problems at their root cause.

Gene Replacement

Gene replacement introduces a healthy version of a faulty gene to restore function.

Gene Silencing

Gene silencing turns off harmful genes to prevent diseases from developing.

Delivery Methods

Gene therapy uses viral and non-viral vectors to deliver therapeutic genes.
